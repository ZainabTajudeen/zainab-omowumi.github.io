Healthcare · WHO Data
Data Analyst

Global Life Expectancy Analysis (2000–2021)

Analyzed 22 years of WHO Global Health Observatory data across 183 countries, examining long-term trends, gender differences, cross-country disparities, and the COVID-19 pandemic's impact on population health.

🌍 Global life expectancy rose from 66.8 (2000) to 72.4 years (2019), a gain of 5.6 years. COVID-19 reversed ~1.5 years by 2021. Women lived 74.1 yrs vs men 67.9 yrs. Japan (84.3) vs Lesotho (50.7): a 33.6-year gap.
